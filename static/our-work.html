Frank taxibot

Frank is a B2B partnership for taxi companies and aggregators. Allows customers to order taxis in their apps around the world. Allows taxi companies to share orders for customer retention. Allows to receive orders from social media. Saves the brands of taxi companies and allows to compete with global aggregators such as Uber, Yandex.Taxi, etc.
